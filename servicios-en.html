Pest Control Treatments

We protect your plants and trees from pests and diseases with effective and environmentally friendly treatments. Our preventive and curative approach ensures the long-term health of your garden.

Specialized treatments:

  • Red palm weevil: Preventive and curative treatment against this dangerous pest
  • Scale insects: Control of cochineal and other scale insects
  • Aphids: Elimination of aphids that damage plant health
  • Fungal diseases: Prevention and treatment of fungi and molds
  • Caterpillars: Control of processionary and other harmful caterpillars

We use eco-friendly products that are safe for your family and pets, while being effective against pests and diseases.
