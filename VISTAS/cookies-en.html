Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to inform about the use of cookies on this website in accordance with the legislation on information society services and electronic commerce.

What are Cookies and what are they used for?

Cookies are text files generated and stored by a web server on the user's device when accessing certain web pages, to store and retrieve information about browsing. They allow obtaining information about navigation, improving user experience, collecting usage statistics, and other information.

How is the use of cookies authorized?

The user authorizes the use of cookies by clicking the "ACCEPT" notice published in the first layer of information. Consent can be revoked at any time by disabling and rejecting their use.

  • YouTube: Web videos.
  • Facebook: Social interaction.

How to reject, disable, and delete cookies?

The user can prevent or reject the use of cookies, disable and delete them at any time regarding their browsing on this website. Most web browsers allow managing preferences regarding the use of cookies. Check your browser's help for more information.
